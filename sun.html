The sun

The Sun or sol, is the star at the center of our solar system and is responsible for the eart,s climate and weather.

The sun is an almost perfect sphere with a difference of just 10km in diameter between the poles and the equator. The average radius of the sunis 695,508 km (109.2 X that of the earth ) of which 20-25% is the core

Sun Facts

  • Age: 4,6 Billion Years
  • Type: Yellow Dwarf (G2V)
  • Diameter: 1,392,684 km
  • Equatorial Circumference: 4,370,005,6 km
  • Mass: 1,99 x 10^30 kg (333,060 Earths)
  • Surface Tempeture: 5,500°c
